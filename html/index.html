E-filing made easy

File990.org imports all relevant data about your organization directly from the IRS database. You just need to know your EIN, and we'll get the return started for you.

  • SECURE with IRS efile
    File990.org uses bank level security to ensure all your personal information is kept safe. As an authorized IRS e-filer, you can rest assured that your return will be submitted securely to the IRS
  • Never file late again
    We'll save your fiscal period and send you a reminder when it's time to file next year's return. We'll save all your data too, making the next filing a breeze.
  • Fast and easy Filling
    File your tax return in minutes instead of hours. Just answer a series of simple questions and we'll do all the heavy lifting.

organizations with multiple chapters or components

Keeping track of hundreds or thousands of components can be a huge undertaking. Our platform automatically tracks filings and sends reminders for you.
  • Never lose exempt status

    File990 let's you filter by components that will lose their exempt status for failing to file this year, making it easy to focus on the groups that need assistance.

    Track component filings

    Import your EIN list to see all your component tax filings in real-time. Easily view filings by year or by organization.

  • Remind components to file

    Each component group will receive an email when it's time to file their return. Treasurers don't even need to know their EIN to file, they just have to click the link to file in the reminder email.

  • Authorized IRS e-filer for forms 990N and 990EZ

    The user experience is extremely simple for your components. They just review the data we provide, answer any remaining questions and click submit.

Having a staff member file 990n forms on behalf of your components can take weeks or months of valuable time each year. Our group-filing system turns that time into minutes.
  • 990N component
    File for all or your 990N component groups at once with our group-filing tool. Instantly receive verification of accepted returns and track any returns that fail.
